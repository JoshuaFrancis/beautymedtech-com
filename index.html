The platform, in detail

  • Multispectral diagnostic

    A 12-channel capture maps pigment, vascular, and collagen depth in under six minutes at chairside.

  • Auto-derived calibration

    Six wavelengths and a single handpiece. Settings are derived from the diagnostic, not entered by hand.

  • Outcomes ledger

    Anonymised, aggregated, and contributed back. Every protocol, every result, at 30, 60, and 90 days.

  • Two-stage confirmation

    The handpiece will not deliver fluence without an active calibration tied to the patient on file.

  • Recovery library

    Topicals matched to each protocol. Peptide-led for collagen, vitamin-C-led for pigment, zinc-led for vascular.

  • Network learning

    Each session is informed by the network’s last ten thousand. Not by the operator’s last ten.
